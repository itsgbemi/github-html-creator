Beyoncé: The Reigning Queen of Reinvention

Born and raised in Houston, Texas, Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter first gained fame in the late '90s as the lead singer of Destiny's Child, one of the best-selling girl groups of all time. But her solo career, which launched with Dangerously in Love in 2003, marked the beginning of her evolution into a global phenomenon. With hits like “Crazy in Love” and “Baby Boy,” she showcased her vocal range, commanding stage presence, and ability to dominate the charts..
